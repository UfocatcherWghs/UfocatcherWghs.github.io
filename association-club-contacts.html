Get involved

Hitterz Volleyball was founded on a simple belief — that teamwork and passion create more than just a team, they create a family. What started as a small group of players coming together for the love of the game has grown into a close-knit community built on trust, dedication, and shared goals. At Hitterz, we play with heart. Every serve, every spike, and every dive reflects our commitment to growth and excellence, both on and off the court. Whether you’re an experienced player or just stepping into the game, we welcome everyone who’s ready to learn, push their limits, and contribute to the energy that defines our club.

Become a Sponsor

Supporting Hitterz Volleyball gives your business the opportunity to make a real impact in the community while gaining valuable exposure. Partnering with Hitterz Volleyball not only strengthens your brand’s reputation but also demonstrates your commitment to fostering local talent and healthy, active lifestyles. Join us in shaping the future of volleyball in Auckland while showcasing your business to an engaged and passionate audience.
