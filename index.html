What is food

"Any nutritious substance that people or animals eat or drink or that plants absorb in order to maintain life and growth."

Ever since we are born we know we need food to survive, because we feel hunger. Good food not only fills our stomach but also our soul.

We need food to survive, it provides us energy and the nutriend we need in order to function. We loose energy through moving, breathing and even just existing (while alive, you lose heat energy). And in order to live you need to regain that energy, which we take by food. (Plus food is too good to not consume.)

Nutrition

The five main food groups are:

  • Fruit and veg
  • Starchy foods
  • Dairy
  • Protein
  • Fat

    • The 6 key nutrients are:

  • Water
  • Protein
  • Fat
  • Carbohydrates
  • Minerals
  • Vitamins

    • It's really important to have a balanced diet with all these food groups. A balanced diet supplies nutrients that your body needs to function efficiently, and so that you can live to your best potential. It also makes you less prone to diseases and illnesses.
